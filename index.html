About AptusHealth

AptusHealth reads the health metrics your Apple Watch already records — heart rate variability (HRV), resting heart rate, sleep, and VO2 max — and turns them into a simple recovery score. Start a workout on your Apple Watch to stream live heart rate to your iPhone and receive AI-driven coaching in real time.

Getting started

  • Install AptusHealth on your iPhone from the App Store or TestFlight.
  • Grant Health access when prompted so the app can read your metrics.
  • Pair your Apple Watch for live workout coaching and real-time heart rate.
  • Start a workout from the app on your Apple Watch to begin live coaching.

AptusHealth is a wellness and fitness tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.
